Welcome to the U17 Rhinos Team Page

The U17 Rhinos are part of our mixed U17 age group, focused on fun, teamwork, and football skills for children aged 16-17.

Sessions are held weekly and matches are played in local NEHYL leagues. All abilities are welcome!
