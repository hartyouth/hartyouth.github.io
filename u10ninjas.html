Welcome to the U10 Ninjas Team Page

The U10 Ninjas are part of our mixed U10 age group, focused on fun, teamwork, and football skills for children aged 9-10.

Sessions are held weekly and matches are played in local NEHYL leagues. All abilities are welcome!
