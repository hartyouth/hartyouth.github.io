Useful things to know about Wildcats

  • How do I book someone on to attend a Wildcats session?
    You will need to book their place online before attending a session. You’ll be able to do this from the search results when you search for your nearest session using the tool above.
  • How long does a Wildcats session last?
    Sessions usually lasts one hour however this may vary so please check with delivering the sessions before attending.
  • How much does it cost to attend a Wildcats session?
    Most sessions charge up to a maximum of £3. Please check those delivering the session though, as they may offer free taster sessions for new girls.
  • Who runs the Wildcats sessions?
    All sessions are delivered by our super friendly, FA qualified coaches. The welfare of your daughter is key, so we ensure that all Wildcats coaches are fully trained in health and safety, safeguarding and more.
  • My daughter needs extra support, can she attend Wildcats?
    Definitely! We believe that football is for everyone and want to ensure that every girl has an opportunity to take part and has a great experience. Get in touch with your local session deliverer and let them know how they can best support.
  • How do I volunteer at a Wildcats session?
    You don’t need to have football genius to volunteer at Wildcats. If you love having fun – follow the link to 'Volunteer at Wildcats' below to find out more about how you can get involved.
  • I want to become a Wildcats provider
    We are always looking to grow our network of Wildcats providers. Reach out to your local County FA to understand how you can get involved with delivering this awesome programme.
