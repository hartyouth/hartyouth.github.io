Welcome to the U7 Typhoons Team Page

The U7 Typhoons are part of our mixed U7 age group, focused on fun, teamwork, and football skills for children aged 6-7.

Sessions are held weekly and matches are played in local leagues. All abilities are welcome!

Team Contact

If you would like to learn more or join, email u7@hartyouthfc.uk
