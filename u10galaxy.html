Welcome to the U10 Galaxy Team Page

The U10 Galaxy are part of our girls U10 age group, focused on fun, teamwork, and football skills for girls aged 9-10.

Sessions are held weekly and matches are played in local NEHYL leagues. All abilities are welcome!
