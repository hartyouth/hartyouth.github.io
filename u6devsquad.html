Welcome to the U6 Dev Squad Team Page

The U6 Dev Squad is our youngest group, for boys and girls in Year R and Year 1, focused on fun, skills, and early football development for children aged 5-6.

Sessions are held every Saturday during term time at Cambridge Primary School, Queens Road, Wellesley, Aldershot GU11 4AA, and are open to all abilities. Our aim is to nurture a love for the game and build confidence in a safe, supportive environment.
