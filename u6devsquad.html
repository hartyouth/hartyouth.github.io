Welcome to the U6 Dev Squad Team Page

The U6 Dev Squad is our youngest group, focused on fun, skills, and early football development for children aged 5-6.

Sessions are held weekly and are open to all abilities. Our aim is to nurture a love for the game and build confidence in a safe, supportive environment.

Team Contact

If you would like to learn more or join, email u6devsquad@hartyouthfc.uk
